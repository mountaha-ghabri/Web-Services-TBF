How does it work?

  • Search Your Book by Title

    To find books, simply enter the title in the search bar. Our system will then display relevant information. For books available at the upcoming book fair, we'll provide details such as booth location, availability dates, and any special offers. For sold-out titles, we'll provide information on the book, including author, publisher, a brief description, and suggest alternative sources where you can purchase it, such as online bookstores or local libraries. We'll also provide an estimated price range for both available and sold-out titles.

  • Bookmark & Keep it for yourself

    The book you're looking for isn't available? No problem! You can book it, request it, and get it ASAP.

  • Read & Enjoy

    Future possibility of online purchase. (with Reading Accessiblity options, and Braille version too !)
